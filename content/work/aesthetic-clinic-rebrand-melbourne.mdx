A well-established aesthetics clinic had outgrown its brand. The treatments and the team had
become genuinely premium; the identity and website still read as a high-street salon. The
mismatch was costing them the very clients they most wanted to attract.

## The brief

Reposition the clinic as the considered, premium choice in a crowded market — without losing
the warmth that made existing clients loyal. Brand, website and the words on every page all
needed to move together.

## What we did

We rebuilt the identity around quiet confidence: a refined wordmark, a warm-neutral palette,
and photography art-directed to feel editorial rather than clinical. The website followed.

- Treatment pages restructured for both genuine education and search
- A booking flow stripped of friction, designed to convert curiosity into appointments
- Compliant, generic treatment language that educates and ranks
- A content engine — our Journal model — to compound in search over time

## The result

Four months after launch, online bookings were up 64% and — more tellingly — the average
treatment value had risen 38% as the new brand drew a different, more considered client. The
clinic finally looked like what it had quietly become.
