## The idea

Private dentistry sells trust by the appointment, so the brand should feel like being given time.

Aven Dental Rooms is a North & Refine concept study: a one-dentist private
practice in Marylebone, where the whole proposition is unhurried appointments,
honest fees and one resident clinician who knows your name. The brief we set
ourselves was to make a dental brand that reads more like a members' room than
a clinic, without hiding what it costs or who does the work.

## The brand

A split composition: the practice's voice on parchment, the patient's outcome in photography.

The identity leans on generous whitespace, a confident lowercase headline
voice, and warm photography cropped tight on genuine smiles rather than
stock perfection. Availability is treated as part of the brand: the next
open appointment sits on the homepage like a quiet promise.

## The experience

Everything a nervous patient checks in the first thirty seconds is on the first screen.

Fees, the dentist's name, patient ratings and the next available time all
surface before a single scroll. The design bets that transparency is the
premium position in private dentistry, and that restraint does more for a
Marylebone address than marble ever could.
