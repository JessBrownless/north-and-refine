## The idea

A skincare service run by consultant dermatologists deserves a brand that feels like the consultation, not the chemist aisle.

selv is a North & Refine concept study, built to explore how our editorial
language translates to consumer dermatology. The premise: prescription
skincare formulated for one person at a time, assessed and reviewed online by
consultant dermatologists. Most brands in this space shout efficacy. The ones
patients trust speak quietly and let the clinicians carry the authority.

## The brand

One lowercase word, a serif with real warmth, and photography close enough to see skin as it is.

The identity pairs an unassuming lowercase wordmark with intimate, honest
portraiture: skin photographed as texture and tone rather than airbrushed to
plastic. A single warm palette holds the darkness of the consultation room
against parchment neutrals, so the interface recedes and the person remains.

## The experience

The homepage reads like a diagnosis, not a shopfront.

A full-bleed portrait, one line of type, and a formulation card that treats
the prescription as the product: reviewed by a named consultant, delivery
dated, nothing to browse. The design assumes a patient arrives anxious and
leaves reassured, which is the same assumption we bring to every practice we
work with.
